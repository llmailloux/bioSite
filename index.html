Air Force

Parker left for bootcamp at Lackland AF base in Texas on April 9th 2019, he graduated June 14th 2019. He was honored for graduating in the top 10% percent of his class, along with receiving Thunderbolt for physical fitness. After graduating and becoming an Airman, he went to Bolix Mississippi for another 12 weeks of training for air traffic control. He is now in Florida at Eglin Air Force Base where he will spend the next 4 years as an Airman in air traffic control.
